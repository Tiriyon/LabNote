Chapter 5 PFA whole -mount larvae fixation

5.1 Materials:

  • 4% PFA
  • 50ml Falcon tube

5.2 Procedure:

  1. Fill 50ml tube with larvae and embryo water, place in ice until larvae sink.
  2. Remove liquid carefully to not lift larvae.
  3. Fill 10ml PFA 4%.
